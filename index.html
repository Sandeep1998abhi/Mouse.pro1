PLAY AT LIGHTSPEED

Now you can game faster and more accurately. The iconic G502 now features superfast LIGHTSPEED 1 ms wireless connectivity. The LIGHTSPEED ecosystem of wireless technologies are trusted by esports professionals to deliver pro-grade, ultra-fast and reliable gaming performance.

HIGH-PERFORMANCE HERO 25K SENSOR

Next-generation optical sensor delivers incredible precision and accuracy with no smoothing, filtering, or acceleration across the entire 25,000 DPI sensitivity range — while also achieving up to 10x the power efficiency (compared to previous generations).

11 PROGRAMMABLE BUTTONS & HYPER-FAST SCROLL WHEEL

G502 LIGHTSPEED is fully equipped with 11 programmable buttons. In G502 LIGHTSPEED, both primary buttons now feature metal-spring tensioning for fast, crisp actuation feel and performance. Customize key binds and macros, for each game and app, via Logitech G HUB software. A lighter Hyper-fast scroll wheel spins freely for fast, fluid scrolling and locks into place for standard notched scrolling.

TUNABLE WEIGHT SYSTEM

The included six adjustable weights (2x4g and 4x2g) can be arranged in a wide variety of configurations inside the G502 to customize the weight and balance of your mouse. This will give you a precision-tuned experience feel for optimal pixel tracking and targeting in your game.
